## Bottleneck Analysis

Check the stage with the worst conversion rate. That's your bottleneck.

| Bottleneck | Likely Cause | Fix |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Low response rate | Generic outreach, bad targeting, deliverability issues | Better personalization, verify ICP fit, check spam score |
| Responses but no meetings | Weak CTA, too aggressive, wrong channel | Soften ask, add value first, try different channel |
| Meetings but no qualification | Talking to wrong people, no pain | Tighten ICP, pre-qualify before calls, ask harder questions |
| Qualified but no proposals | Slow follow-up, unclear next steps | Same-day proposal after qualification call |
| Proposals but no closes | Pricing issues, no urgency, weak proposal | Better anchoring, add guarantee, create forcing event |

---

## Weekly Review Questions

1. Where are deals stalling? What's the common thread?
2. Which channel is producing the best ROI on time spent?
3. Am I talking to decision-makers or influencers?
4. What objections keep coming up? Are they in my pre-handle matrix?
5. Which closed deals can generate referrals or case studies this week?
6. Am I spending >60% of selling time on qualified prospects (BANT 70+)?
7. What's my speed-to-lead? Am I responding within 5 minutes?
