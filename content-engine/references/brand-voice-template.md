## Enforcement Rules

When Content Engine generates content, it will:
1. Check every output against this config before finalizing
2. Flag any violations of banned words/topics
3. Score tone alignment on the spectrum scales above
4. Ensure CTA style matches preferred formats
5. Verify visual concepts match brand colors/fonts
6. Compare output against calibration samples for voice match

Violations will appear in `brand-voice-check.md` with suggested fixes.
