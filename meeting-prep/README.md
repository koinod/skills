**Walk into every meeting as the most prepared person in the room.**

Free AI skill that auto-generates executive-quality meeting briefings in 60 seconds.

## Quick Start

```
/meeting-prep Stripe John.Collison sales
/meeting-prep Acme Corp jane.doe partner
```

## What You Get (Free)

- Company snapshot (stage, size, revenue, recent news)
- Person brief (background, career, communication style, decision authority)
- Key intel (3-5 things that matter)
- Talking points tailored to THEM
- Questions to ask (high-value, rapport, strategic, closing)
- Landmines and objection handling
- Recommended meeting arc (open to close)

- **Partner** — Alignment mapping, mutual incentives, integration points
