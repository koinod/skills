# Objection Destroyer Scorecard

## Drill Scoring (per response)

| Dimension | Weight | 1 (Miss) | 3 (Solid) | 5 (Destroyer) |
|-----------|--------|----------|-----------|----------------|
| **Speed** | 15% | >15 sec to respond | 5-15 sec | <5 sec |
| **Classification** | 15% | Wrong type | Right type, missed subtype | Type + subtype + severity correct |
| **Framework Selection** | 20% | Wrong framework | Acceptable framework | Optimal framework for scenario |
| **Tone Match** | 15% | Generic / one-size-fits-all | Partially adapted | Perfectly mirrors buyer style |
| **Response Quality** | 20% | Weak / defensive / begging | Competent handling | Closes or advances with confidence |
| **Follow-Up** | 10% | No next step mentioned | Vague next step | Specific close or concrete next action |
| **Ethics** | 5% | Manipulative / dishonest | Neutral | Genuinely serves the prospect |

**Score Ranges**:
- **4.5-5.0**: DESTROYER -- You're closing deals others walk away from
- **3.5-4.4**: CLOSER -- Solid. Drill weak spots to level up
- **2.5-3.4**: DEVELOPING -- Good instincts, needs framework discipline
- **1.0-2.4**: ROOKIE -- Keep drilling. 10 reps/day minimum
