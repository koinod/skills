# Pricing Psychology Basics

## 3-Tier Anchor Model
- 70% of buyers choose the middle option (Goldilocks effect)
- Top tier exists to make middle tier look reasonable
- Ratios: 0.7x / 1.0x (recommended) / 2.5x

## Key Principles
- Use "investment" not "cost"
- Specific numbers ($47,832) beat round numbers ($48,000)
- Never discount -- add bonuses to increase perceived value
- 14-day proposal validity creates natural urgency
