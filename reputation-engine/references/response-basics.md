# Response Framework Basics

## HEARD (Negative Reviews)
Hear > Empathize > Apologize > Resolve > Direct (offline)

## AMPLIFY (Positive Reviews)
Appreciate > Mirror > Personalize > Link > Invite > Feature > Your signature

## BRIDGE (Neutral Reviews)
Begin with thanks > Recognize > Inquire > Demonstrate > Give reason to return > End open
